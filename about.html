About Me

Hello, my name is Jake Duus. I am the Fleet and Grounds Manager at Trinity Bible College in Ellendale, North Dakota. I oversee the vehicles and the landscape that keep the campus running smoothly.

I’m also a part-time student at the college, continuing to grow in knowledge and skill while balancing work, school, and life. Before coming to Trinity, I served in the military, where I learned the value of discipline, teamwork, and perseverance—lessons that continue to shape who I am today.

My life is rooted in a strong faith in Christ and guided by a sense of honor and duty to both country and community. I believe in giving my best to every task, living by the words: “Whatever your hand finds to do, do it with all your might.”

When I’m not working, you’ll likely find me enjoying cars, motorcycles, the outdoors, traveling with my wife, or cooking ribs on the grill.
